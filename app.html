What this page is

A simple app that supports patients already in a study.

This is not a complex clinical dashboard. It is a calm, patient-friendly companion designed to make trial participation easier to understand.

🧾

Study overview

Patients can enter an NCT number and see a simple overview connected to their study.

❓

Helpful FAQs

Common questions are answered in clear language so patients feel less lost during participation.

🌐

Easy access

The app keeps key information simple and gives patients a direct path back to the main website.

Inside the app

Simple, direct, and reassuring.

The app experience is intentionally light. Patients enter their name, email, and NCT number, then access a simple dashboard with the most useful information in one place.

1

Enter basic information

Name, email, and NCT number are enough to start the journey.

2

Open the dashboard

The patient sees a welcome screen and quick shortcuts to the most useful areas.

3

Stay informed

Study overview, FAQs, and future support features are all designed to stay simple.

App structure

Minimal by design.

The goal is not to overwhelm the patient. The goal is to provide calm, practical guidance in a format that feels easy to use.

Landing Name · Email · NCT Number
Main screen Hello, [Patient Name] + 3 shortcut icons: My Trial, FAQs, Website
Future expansion Reminders, study-specific content, and sponsor-supported features can be added later.
How it fits

The website educates. The app accompanies.

My Trial Journey begins on the website, where patients learn and explore. The app comes later, when a patient is already participating and needs a simple companion.
