Why attend?

Why these webinars matter

The goal is not to overwhelm patients with technical language. The goal is to give them clarity, confidence, and better questions.

Clear explanations

Sessions are designed in language that patients and families can actually understand.

More confidence

Better understanding can reduce fear and help people approach conversations more prepared.

Community support

Webinars can help patients and caregivers feel less alone while learning about a complex topic.
